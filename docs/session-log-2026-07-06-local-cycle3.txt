--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
KEY DECISIONS & FINDINGS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- AUC-based threshold (0.75) validated: the gold pair score distribution has
  median 0.753 and the shuffle (random) distribution has median 0.374 — clean
  separation. At 0.75, zero false positives from shuffled pairs.
- The matcher from git (homophone_score) matches the stage4_filter combo
  (0.5*ngram + 0.5*feat) exactly — the judge is consistent, not dodgy.
- EN paraphraser quality: 91% token accuracy, errors mostly morphological
  over-generation (rapid→speed instead of quick) — benign for bridges.
- FR paraphraser quality: 92% accuracy, but Qwen3-4B is English-dominant
  causing ~3% cross-language leakage (doux→"soft" in French output).
  Retraining with Mistral-7B (French-native) in progress to fix this.
- DUAL translation edges are the bottleneck for bridges: 102,772 edges haven't
  grown because they come from original DUAL-A/B tiers. More DUAL pairs =
  more pivot synonymy = more meaning-bridges.
- Flywheel confirmed across 3 cycles: each cycle's discoveries expand the
  gold, bringing new neighborhoods into the next cycle. Diminishing returns
  signal "vein mining out" — next growth comes from GPU (paraphraser
  generalizing beyond corpus edges).
